一道有意思的级数重排问题

发表于2022-08-15|更新于2022-08-21|分析学数学分析级数
|字数总计:1.2k|阅读时长:6分钟|评论数:

之前在知乎上回答的一个级数重排的问题。

问题: 设\(0<\alpha<1\), 将级数 \(\sum\limits_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^{n-1}}{n^{\alpha}}\) 按照每 \(p\) 个正项后为 \(q\) 个负项的规则进行重排, 但保持正项之间和负项之间的原有顺序, 证明：重排后的级数当且仅当 \(p=q\) 时收敛.

此证明在一个月所答, 起初想到的是用莱布尼茨判别法证明充分性,用柯西判别法来反证必要性，证明方法位于文后的(题目另证)。现在深入学习级数理论与积分理论，我有了另外的证明方法。

第一种方法引理1参考了李杨强化讲义及证明思路部分参考知乎第一楼回答；第二种方法为个人所想, 笔者学识不才, 如有疑问与指正请留言。

引理1: 若 \(\lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty} u_{n}=0\), 且\(S_{n}=\sum\limits_{k=1}^{n} u_{k}\)有一个子列 \(S_{p n}\)(\(p\)为固定整数）收敛, 则 \(\sum\limits_{n=1}^{\infty} u_{n}\)收敛。

证明: (可左滑,电脑阅读效果最佳) \[ \bbox[#F5F5F5,10px] { \begin{array}{l} \lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty} S_{n} 存在 \Longleftrightarrow \lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty} S_{pn+q}(q=0,1,2, \cdots, p-1) 存在且相等\\ \\ 而 \lim \limits_{n \rightarrow \infty} S_{p n+q}=\lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty} S_{p n}+\lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty}\left(u_{p n+1}+\cdots+u_{p n+q}\right)=\lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty} S_{p n}\\ \\ 即 \sum\limits_{n=1}^{\infty} u_{n}=\lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty} S_{n}=\lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty} S_{p n} \end{array} } \]

引理2: \(\lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty} n^{\alpha-1} \cdot \sum\limits_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{k^{\alpha}}=\frac{1}{1-\alpha} \quad(\alpha \neq 1)\)，即\(\sum\limits_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{k^\alpha}=\frac{n^{1-\alpha}}{1-\alpha}+\alpha_{n}\left(\lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty} \alpha_{n}=0\right)\)

证明: \[\bbox[#F5F5F5,10px] { \begin{array}{l} \lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty} n^{\alpha-1} \cdot \sum\limits_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{k^{\alpha}}=\lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty} \frac{1}{n} \sum\limits_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{\left(\frac{k}{n}\right)^{\alpha}}=\int_{0}^{1} \frac{1}{x^{\alpha}} d x=\frac{1}{1-\alpha} \end{array} }\]

题目证明:

\[ \bbox[#F5F5F5,10px] { \begin{array}{l} \begin{aligned} S_{(p+q)n}& =\left(\frac{1}{1^{\alpha}}+\frac{1}{3^{\alpha}}+\cdots+\frac{1}{\left.(2p-1)^{\alpha}\right.}\right)-\left(\frac{1}{2^{\alpha}}+\cdots+\frac{1}{(2 q)^{\alpha}}\right)+\cdots+ \\ & \left[\frac{1}{[2(n-1) p+1]^{\alpha}}+\cdots+\frac{1}{(2 p n-1)^{\alpha}}\right]-\left[\frac{1}{[2(q(n-1)+1)]^{\alpha}}+\cdots+\frac{1}{(2 q n)^{\alpha}}\right] \\ & =\sum_{k=1}^{p n} \frac{1}{(2 k-1)^{\alpha}}-\sum_{k=1}^{q n} \frac{1}{(2 k)^{\alpha}} \\ & =\sum_{k=1}^{2pn} \frac{1}{k^{\alpha}}-\sum_{k=1}^{p n} \frac{1}{(2 k)^{\alpha}}-\sum_{k=1}^{q n} \frac{1}{(2 k)^{\alpha}} \\ & =\sum_{k=1}^{2pn} \frac{1}{k^{\alpha}}-\frac{1}{2^ \alpha}\left(\sum_{k=1}^{p n} \frac{1}{k^{\alpha}}+\sum_{k=1}^{q n} \frac{1}{k^{\alpha}}\right) \\ &=\frac{(2pn)^{1-\alpha}}{1-\alpha}+\alpha_n-\frac{1}{2^\alpha}[\frac{(pn)^{1-\alpha}}{1-\alpha}+\frac{(qn)^{1-\alpha}}{1-\alpha}+\beta_n+\gamma_n]\\ & =\frac{n^{1-\alpha}}{2^{\alpha}(1-\alpha)}\left(p^{1-\alpha}-q^{1-\alpha}\right)+\alpha_{n}-\frac{1}{2^\alpha}\left(\beta_{n}+r_{n}\right) \end{aligned}\\ \\ 由0\lt \alpha \lt 1知\lim\limits _{n \rightarrow \infty}n^{1-\alpha}=+\infty , 则\sum\limits_{n=1}^{\infty} u_{n}收敛\Leftrightarrow S_{(p+q)n}收敛\Leftrightarrow p=q \end{array} }\]

题目另证： \[ \bbox[#F5F5F5,10px] { \begin{array}{l} \begin{array}{l} 令 a_{2 m-1}=\frac{1}{[(2 m-2) p+1]^{\alpha}}+\frac{1}{[(2 m-2) p+3]^{\alpha}}+\cdots+\frac{1}{[2 m p-1]^{\alpha}}, a_{2 m}=\frac{1}{[(2 m-2) q+2]^{\alpha}}+\frac{1}{\left[(2 m -2) q+4]^{\alpha}\right.}+\cdots+\frac{1}{(2 m q)^{\alpha}} \end{array}\\ \\ 必要性： 若重排级数收敛且 p \neq q, 不妨设 p>q\\ \\ \begin{aligned} m>1 时, \left|a_{2 m}-a_{2 m-1}\right| &=\mid \frac{1}{[(2 m-2) q+1]^{\alpha}}-\frac{1}{[(2m-2) p+1]^{\alpha}}+\cdots+\frac{1}{[(2 m-2) q]^{\alpha}}-\frac{1}{\left[(2m-2)q-1]^{\alpha}\right.}+\cdots+\frac{1}{[2 mp-1]^{\alpha}}| \\ &>\frac{1}{[(2m-2)q+1]^{\alpha}}+\cdots+\frac{1}{(2 mp-1))^{\alpha}}>\frac{1}{(2 m p-1)^{\alpha}} \\ \end{aligned}\\ \\ \begin{aligned} \Rightarrow\left|S_{4 m+4}-S_{2 m}\right| &=\mid\left(a_{4m+4}-a_{4m+3}\right)+\cdots+\left(a_{2 m+2}-a_{2 m+1} \mid\right.\\ & \geqslant\left|a_{4 m+4}-a_{4m+3}\right|+\cdots+\left|a_{2 m+2}-a_{2 m+1}\right| \\ &>\frac{m+1}{(4 m+3)^{\alpha}}=\frac{1}{4^{\alpha}} \cdot \frac{m+1}{\left(m+\frac{1}{4}\right)^{\alpha}}>\frac{1}{4^{\alpha}} \end{aligned}\\ \\ \begin{aligned} 取 \varepsilon_{0}=\frac{1}{4^{\alpha}}, \forall m, \exists n_{1}=4 m+4>m, n_{2}=2 m>m 时, 有 \left|S_{4 m+4}-S_{2 m}\right|>\frac{1}{4^{\alpha}}=\varepsilon_{0} 即级数发散, 与命题矛盾, 则 p=q.\\ \end{aligned}\\ \\ \begin{array}{l} 充分性：若 p=q,a_{2 m-1}-a_{2 m}>0, a_{2 m}-a_{2 m+1}>0 \Rightarrow\left\{a_{m}\right\}严格递减, \lim \limits_{m \rightarrow \infty} a_{2 m-1}=\lim\limits _{m \rightarrow \infty} a_{2 m}=0 (p, q有限),由莱布尼茨判别法知收敛. \end{array} \end{array} }\]
